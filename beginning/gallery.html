Our Chief Zee on Master Classes
Read More

If you simply want to master the art of making New York-style pizza at home, two things are key: knowing how to properly roll out the dough and finding the perfect balance of flavors for the sauce. Our Chef Zee will explain the process of dough preparation and fermentation—specifically, understanding the principles of hydration, the characteristics of different flour varieties, and proofing times. He will demonstrate how to make the sauce from scratch by blending crushed tomatoes, fresh herbs, and spices. Finally, he will cover the dough-rolling and pizza assembly stages, as well as the specific techniques required for baking at high temperatures.
