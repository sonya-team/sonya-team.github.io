Our history
Read More

Pizzeria Del Cerro Pizza & Beer is a relatively new, locally owned eatery in the Del Cerro neighborhood of San Diego, bringing New York-style pies and craft beer to the community. Opened in late 2024, the pizzeria was established by a collaborative duo: entrepreneur and business graduate Sadeer Oraha and experienced pizza-maker Zee. Zee brought over 16 years of experience crafting New York-style pizza, while Sadeer focused on operations and strategic growth. The Space & PartnershipThe pizzeria took over a former taco shop space within the Windmill Farms Market plaza (located at 6358 Del Cerro Blvd). This was part of an expansion by the family behind Keg N Bottle, a well-known San Diego liquor and beverage chain established in 1981 by the late Najib Konja. The Konja family utilized their existing beverage expertise to help curate a strong tap list for the new pizzeria, transforming the space into a cozy hangout.

Company Overview

Del Cerro Pizza & Beer is a local, family-owned restaurant located at 6358 Del Cerro Blvd, San Diego, CA 92120. Founded by business partners Sadir and Zi, the pizzeria opened its doors in late 2024 or early 2025. The entrepreneurs transformed a former taco shop into a cozy establishment, perfectly suited for family outings and gatherings with neighbors. The restaurant specializes in New York-style pizza, crafted by Zi—a master with over 16 years of experience in pizza making and the catering industry. The kitchen prioritizes fresh, high-quality ingredients, and the dough is prepared using a special technique designed to achieve a signature crispy crust.

Our Goals

Our pizzeria has ambitious plans for growth, coupled with a deep commitment to the interests of its neighborhood. Our core development and operational objectives include: Community-Oriented Growth—our primary goal is to remain a genuine, family-friendly local hub for residents, consistently responding to the needs of our neighbors and contributing to the life of the community; Menu Quality and Consistency—by focusing on a streamlined menu, we ensure that every pizza, appetizer (antipasti), and dessert consistently meets the highest standards of quality; and Technological Convenience—leveraging delivery platforms such as Slice to optimize our order processing workflow and ensure a seamless delivery experience for our customers.
